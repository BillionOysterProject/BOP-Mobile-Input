Measuring Oyster Growth

Directions: 10 substrate shells in a cage will be tagged with engraved plastic number tags (1-10). Each tagged shell will have multiple live oysters growing on it and increasingly multiple dead oysters (open boxes). Only tagged shells and only live oysters should be measured. Measure only and all live oysters on a given tagged shell. Measure 100 live oysters or 10 tagged shells, whichever comes first. If a shell has more than 12 oysters growing on it, measure only 12 at random.
